Welcome to Sky Schema!

This is our Complex Engineering Project (CEP) of DBMS (Database Management Systems) made by a group of four dedicated members. Our project focuses on booking and managing flights all over the world.

Sky Schema aims to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for travelers to book their flights with ease. We understand the complexities involved in the airline industry, and our project leverages the power of DBMS to simplify the flight booking process.

With Sky Schema, you can explore various destinations, check flight availability, compare fares, and make hassle-free bookings. Our platform integrates with multiple airlines, ensuring that you have access to a wide range of flight options to choose from.
